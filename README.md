Corporate KYC & AML Monitoring System

Project Overview
The Corporate KYC & AML Monitoring System is a relational database project developed using MySQL to support Know Your Customer (KYC) and Anti-Money Laundering (AML) compliance processes. The system centralizes company onboarding information, ownership structures, Ultimate Beneficial Owner (UBO) details, transaction monitoring, sanctions screening, and document management.

Business Objective
Financial institutions and compliance teams must verify customer identities, monitor ownership structures, track high-risk transactions, and perform sanctions screening to meet regulatory requirements. This project provides a structured database solution to manage and analyze compliance-related information efficiently.

Features
Company onboarding and profile management
Ownership and shareholder tracking
Ultimate Beneficial Owner (UBO) management
Transaction monitoring and risk flag analysis
Document tracking with submission and expiry dates
Sanctions list management
Relational database design with foreign key constraints
SQL-based reporting and compliance analysis
Database Schema

The project consists of the following tables:
companies – Company master information
ownership – Ownership and shareholder details
ubo_details – Ultimate Beneficial Owner information
documents – Compliance documents and validity tracking
transactions – Transaction monitoring records
sanctions_list – Sanctions screening reference data
